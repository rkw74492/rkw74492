Weekly Schedule

Monday

Morning Yoga Class: Start your week with a relaxing yoga session. (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM)

Live Acoustic Music: Enjoy soulful acoustic performances by local artists. (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Tuesday

Coffee Tasting Workshop: Learn about different coffee brewing methods and taste various specialty coffees. (6:00 PM - 7:30 PM)

Open Mic Night: Showcase your talent or enjoy the performances of local musicians, poets, and comedians. (8:00 PM - 10:00 PM)

Wednesday

Art Exhibition: Explore the works of local artists displayed throughout the coffee shop. (All day)

Trivia Night: Test your knowledge and compete with friends in a fun-filled trivia competition. (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Thursday

Cooking Class: Join our skilled chef and learn to prepare delicious desserts. Limited spots available, reservations required. (5:00 PM - 7:00 PM)

Board Game Night: Gather with friends and enjoy a variety of board games while sipping your favorite drinks. (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Friday

Live Jazz Band: Relax and unwind with the smooth tunes of a live jazz band. (7:30 PM - 9:30 PM)

Saturday

Family Day: Bring your kids for a special day of face painting, storytelling, and fun activities. (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

Poetry Slam: Express your creativity or listen to passionate poets share their work during our poetry slam event. (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Sunday

Book Club Meeting: Discuss the latest book selection with fellow book enthusiasts over a cup of coffee. (3:00 PM - 4:30 PM)

Sunday Brunch: Indulge in a delicious brunch menu accompanied by live acoustic music. (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)

Please note that specific event dates and times may vary, and it's always a good idea to check with the coffee shop for the most up-to-date information and any additional events they may offer.
