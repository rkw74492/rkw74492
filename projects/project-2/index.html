What are shinies?

Hi all! I'm Calypso, and I'm a Pokémon trainer- a Pokémon master, even! I'm on the fast track to completing the PokéDex, and I've fought in plenty of league championship matches.
But that's not important!!
This is my partner, Iyaqua the psyduck (ignore the getup - we were at a Hisuian history re-enactment event in Sinnoh). You might notice that he's a bit different from most psyduck- he's shiny!!
Perhaps you're interested in getting a special Pokémon too- that's what this site is for. I'm here to teach you about shinies and how to find them!
